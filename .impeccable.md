## Design Context

### Users
Clients in Mauritius who are browsing on mobile first and want to understand the salon quickly, trust the business, and book with minimal friction. Their primary job is to scan services, feel confident in the brand, and contact the salon through WhatsApp or phone without confusion.

### Brand Personality
Calm, feminine, meticulous. The tone should feel warm, soft, editorial, and high-end without becoming flashy, loud, or overly luxurious in a fake way.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light editorial salon design built around the existing Sim's Hair and Beauty logo. The experience should feel airy, elegant, and logo-led, using black, deep red, cream, and soft grey with red reserved for accents and booking actions. Anti-references: dark aggressive gradients, generic beauty templates, cluttered card grids, cheap iconography, and anything that feels obviously AI-generated.

### Design Principles
1. Let the logo and typography carry the identity more than decoration.
2. Prefer calm contrast, soft surfaces, and generous spacing over heavy blocks.
3. Keep booking obvious, but never repetitive or pushy.
4. Make each section serve one distinct purpose with minimal filler copy.
5. Aim for an editorial salon feel rather than a boxed landing-page template.
